Meet the Candidate

A Leader Born for Transformation

Hon. Korite Michael Kalango is a dedicated son of Nembe Constituency II, driven by an unyielding passion for sustainable development and inclusive growth.

With deep roots in the community and a rich history of public service, Kalango understands the unique challenges facing Nembe. He represents a new generation of leadership—one that is transparent, accountable, and intensely focused on youth empowerment, economic revitalization, and structural development.

His mission as your representative in the Bayelsa State House of Assembly is to amplify the voices of the voiceless, advocate for fair resource allocation, and foster a thriving ecosystem where every citizen has the opportunity to succeed.

Track Record

Proven Impact

Leadership is not about titles; it's about results. Here is a glimpse of what Kalango has already achieved for our people.

Educational Empowerment

Initiated scholarship schemes providing full tuition support to over 200 underprivileged students across Nembe Constituency II, ensuring that brilliant minds are not left behind due to financial constraints.

Youth Skills Acquisition

Partnered with local NGOs and tech hubs to train over 500 youths in high-demand digital skills and vocational trades, equipping them with startup kits to launch their own small businesses.

Community Health Outreaches

Facilitated multiple medical outreach programs providing free screenings, medications, and minor surgeries to over 1,500 rural dwellers, significantly improving basic healthcare access.

Clean Water Projects

Championed the installation of solar-powered boreholes in remote communities, alleviating the chronic lack of potable water and reducing waterborne diseases.

My Pledge to Nembe

The 2027 Manifesto

A comprehensive, actionable, and sustainable blueprint to elevate Nembe Constituency II through effective legislative representation and grassroots development.

Education & Human Capital

  • Expand scholarship programs for indigent students in tertiary institutions.
  • Advocate for the upgrade of educational infrastructure and modern learning tools.
  • Partner with tech hubs to introduce STEM and digital skills training.

Healthcare & Well-being

  • Sponsor bills to ensure primary healthcare centers are fully equipped and staffed.
  • Implement periodic free medical outreach programs targeting rural communities.
  • Promote maternal and child health initiatives to reduce mortality rates.

Youth & Women Empowerment

  • Facilitate access to micro-credit schemes and grants for women-led SMEs.
  • Launch vocational training centers focusing on technical trades and agriculture.
  • Champion affirmative action policies ensuring active participation in governance.

Agriculture & Blue Economy

  • Support local farmers and fisherfolks with modern tools, fertilizers, and boats.
  • Attract state and federal agricultural interventions to Nembe Constituency II.
  • Foster the processing of local produce to create value chains and employment.

Infrastructure Development

  • Relentlessly lobby the state government to complete abandoned road projects.
  • Facilitate the provision of clean, potable water via solar-powered systems.
  • Push for rural electrification and the expansion of the national grid.

Peace, Security & Inclusion

  • Collaborate with traditional rulers to maintain peace in the constituency.
  • Organize regular town-hall meetings for transparent feedback and inclusion.
  • Establish a constituency liaison office accessible to every citizen.
